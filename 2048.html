AI 2048

Presentation of the project

An artificial intelligence using Monte-Carlo's method to play 2048, a single-player sliding tile puzzle video game.

objectives

Create an AI program better than me at the game.

difficulties

It was my first AI program and I have only my personnal computer, so my computing power was limited.

Result

The AI is not bad, it achieve to get a 2048 tile almost everytime, but due to the limited computing power it is still not the better than me. If I upgrade it significantly, it will take several hours (maybe days) per try.
