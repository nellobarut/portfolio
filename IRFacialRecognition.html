IR facial recognition

Presentation of the project

This program is like Face ID but it works with infrared rays and not visible rays.

objectives

This project was part of my TIPE project, an introduction to research in the scientific domain at the end of the second year of study in engineering. I chose to create, from scratch, a program of facial recognition using only a thermal camera and python. The objectives were multiple, have a good grade, learn about thermal camera and infrared rays and improve my python level.

difficulties

This is probably the project were I overcame the most difficulties. I had to work fast, I needed to create a database of student from school, automatically crop pictures and find a working comparison method.

Result

After more than 6 months of hard work and several different approaches of the problem, I used FFT and the natural extension comparison method and I finally managed to achieve a 90% success rate for the program.
