Wind Farm

Presentation of the project

A simulation of wind farm with data from a wind tunnel and Global Wind Atlas

objectives

I did this group project for school, I was responsible of the python part of the project. The objectvies were to improve my skill with matplotlib and csv file interaction, we also wanted to have a good grade.

difficulties

It was difficult to create a satisfaying polynomial regression without using the one provide by the library numpy.

Result

We manage to have a program with consistent result in term of energy production, and the graphic way we presented the result were great too. On the picture higher black dot are wind turbine, red rectangle are the safety zone too close to wind turbines to put an other one and blue cone are trails created by the wind turbines.
